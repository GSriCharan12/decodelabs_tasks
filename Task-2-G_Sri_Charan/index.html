Who We Are

Crafting architectural narratives in raw materials.

Founded in 2014, LINEA is a design collective of architects, interior stylists, and landscape innovators. We reject the unnecessary, choosing instead to focus on clean geometry, soft lighting, and functional layout.

We believe that architecture is not just building structures, but framing natural light and nature's surrounding environment.

Our Services

Tailored architectural solutions

We guide each project meticulously from original sketch to key hand-off, ensuring our design principles shine through every detail.

01

Architectural Planning

Conceptual planning, volumetric analysis, blueprints, structural engineering, and building permit acquisition for modern residential and commercial designs.

02

Interior Architecture

Bespoke cabinetry design, warm atmospheric lighting schemes, material selection, custom furniture curation, and ergonomic space optimization.

03

Sustainable Landscaping

Designing outdoor gardens, courtyards, and terraces that integrate seamlessly with the indoor architecture, utilizing local materials and water-efficient planting.

04

Turnkey Construction

On-site quality monitoring, contractor supervision, timeline coordination, budget management, and client representation from groundwork to absolute completion.
